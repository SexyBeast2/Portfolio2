About Me

The titular name of this page is in reference to the creator of the page, myself. I am a Nicaraguan-Serb and essentially come down from a line of war babies with a pallate that has evolutionarily adapted to only recognize three tastes: meat, bread, and blood. I only make my meal choices based on their macronutritional values and for the longest time I used to think gourmet was a word used to describe experimental film genres. I used to tell people that I thought Hal Hartley was way more gourmet than David Lynch.

Because of my inherited body-mind composition people have assumed me and my family as being simple minded. However, I just think we're incredibly efficient and in spite of the wider breadth of cultural perspective that has winnowed from the war-torn skies of my two countries into my head, my brain refuses to retain any block of useless information. This is why Serbia has more monasteries than libraries - we're always looking at the bigger picture. Thus, I believe existence precedes essence, and I only engage with what I love, including coding, basketball, and acronyms that define my system of beliefs such as D.E.S.T.I.N.Y. (Digest, Eat, Sex To Immortalize Nicaragua and Yugoslavia).

Work

I'm currently working as a project coordinator for the Homeless Advocacy Project (HAP) where I am helping digitize and update our resource manual into an online directory for people who are homeless or qualified as low-income in the city of San Francisco. I'm also working on a certification in Full-Stack Development through UC Berkeley's Extension program.
